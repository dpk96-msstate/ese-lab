Our Vision

A future in which software systems are built on well-understood, creative, and secure requirements that are systematically validated through empirical evidence.

Our Mission

SERA Lab is developing empirically grounded methods and tools that support creative requirements engineering and strengthen connections between requirements, testing, and security.
